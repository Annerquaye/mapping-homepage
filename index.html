Below are some of my works

Race to the White House: Recreating the map of the US 2020 elections results

This project contains maps that detail how the Democrats swept through the country to win the race to the White House in the keenly contested US Presidential elections in 2020. Tools used: mapbox, javascript, pandas, geojson


A map of dominant economic activities at county and state level

This map uses data from the United States Department of Agriculture to visualizes the main economic structure of counties. Tools used: mapbox, javascript, pandas, geojson


Mapping Citi Bikes rides September 2020,2021

The visualization made use of data on the station names where the trips originated as well as where the trips ended. The location of these stations, provided in the data, was converted into GEOJSON using geopandas library in python. Tools used: mapbox, javascript, pandas, geojson


Central Africa remains dark despite vast energy potential

This uses Natural Earth projection on Mapbox to visualize electricty supply accross the globe. Tools used: mapbox, javascript, pandas, geojson, altair, adobe illustrator
