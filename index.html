Race to the White House: Recreating the map of the US 2020 elections results

This project contains maps that detail how the Democrats swept through the country to win the race to the White House in the keenly contested US Presidential elections in 2020. Tools used: mapbox, javascript, pandas, geojson


A map of dominant economic activities at county and state level

This map uses data from the United States Department of Agriculture to visualizes the main economic structure of counties. Tools used: mapbox, javascript, pandas, geojson
